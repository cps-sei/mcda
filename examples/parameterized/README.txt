Prerequisites: Install MCMT (v2.1 or later) and Cubicle (v0.5 or
later). Make sure "mcmt" and "cubicle" are on your PATH.

MCMT webpage: http://users.mat.unimi.it/users/ghilardi/mcmt
Cubicle webpage: http://cubicle.lri.fr

Our experiments were done with MCMT v2.1 and Cubicle v0.5. To repeat
experiments, run script "./run-exp.sh" without any arguments and
follow instructions.
